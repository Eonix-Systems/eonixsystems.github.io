Built for Builders.

Eonix Systems is an engineering-first hardware company. We prioritize correctness, hardware safety, and clarity over convenience or feature count.

The Problem

Engineers building real machines — robots, drones, industrial automation — serve two masters: Safety and Complexity.

Existing power solutions are either "dumb" components (regulators, fuses) that hide state, or expensive lab equipment that cannot be embedded. This leaves a gap where critical infrastructure is treated as an afterthought.

Our Vision

We believe power infrastructure should be **intelligent infrastructure**. It should self-diagnose, self-protect, and communicate.

Our goal is to build the standard electrical backbone for the next generation of autonomous hardware — from university rovers to industrial AMRs.

Designed and Engineered in India. Built for the global engineering community.
