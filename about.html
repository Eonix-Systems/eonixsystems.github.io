About Eonix Systems

Eonix Systems is an engineering-first hardware company focused on building robust, programmable power electronics for real-world systems.

We believe power electronics should be predictable, safe, and fully observable — not opaque subsystems hidden behind abstractions.

Our approach

We design systems from first principles, prioritizing correctness, hardware safety, and clarity over convenience or feature count.

Built for engineers

Our products are intended for engineers building robots, automation platforms, and embedded systems where failure is expensive and behavior must be understood.

Eonix Systems is built in India, with a focus on contributing high-quality engineering hardware to the global ecosystem.
