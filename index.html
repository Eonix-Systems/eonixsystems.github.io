Power is system-critical infrastructure.

Power electronics define reliability, safety, and performance. Treating power as a black box leads to unpredictable behavior and hidden failure modes.

Eonix platform expose power behavior clearly and enforce safety in hardware to ensure correct operation in complex systems.

Built with intent

SAFETY

Protection mechanisms operate in analog hardware. Software configures within limits that are embedded in hardware.

CONTROL

Fast analog control loops handle regulation, while digital layers provide configuration and telemetry.

VISIBILITY

Voltage, current, power, and fault states are always visible. Power behavior is measured, logged, and reported.
