Intelligent Control

Automatic source selection based on voltage levels, priority assignment, and health status.

Why Active OR-ing?

Traditional passive diodes burn power as heat (Vf ~0.7V). Our Active OR-ing controller uses N-Channel MOSFETs to reduce voltage drop to near zero, enabling high-current redundancy without the thermal penalty.

• Reverse Current Blocking: Prevents battery backfeeding into weak sources.

• Source Prioritization: Intelligently drain auxiliary batteries before main packs.

• Oscillation Dampening: Hysteresis prevents rapid switching between similar sources.
