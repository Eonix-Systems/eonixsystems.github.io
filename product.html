Smart Programmable Power Distribution System

A modular, programmable power distribution system built for embedded hardware, robotics, and engineering systems.

This is not a fixed-function power supply. It is a configurable power platform with hardware-enforced safety, per-channel control, and real-time telemetry.

What This System Is

  • Embeddable power distribution layer for complete systems
  • Multiple independently controlled output channels
  • Designed to live inside robots, rigs, and deployed hardware

What It Enables

  • Deterministic power behavior under varying loads
  • Safe operation during short-circuits and fault conditions
  • Remote configuration and control of power channels
  • Continuous visibility into system power health

Design Philosophy

  • Layered protection using fast analog and supervisory digital control
  • Programmable CC/CV operation where applicable
  • Per-channel isolation to contain faults locally
  • Observability-first design with always-on telemetry

System Scope

  • Power distribution, not just regulation
  • Control and monitoring integrated at the hardware level
  • Designed as infrastructure, not a lab accessory

Detailed electrical specifications, voltage and current limits, channel configurations, and interface documentation are provided in the Technical documentation.
