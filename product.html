Eonix Flow

Eonix Flow is the intelligent power path controller of the Eonix ecosystem. It actively manages multiple power sources using ideal diode OR-ing, ensuring seamless transitions, reverse-current protection, and uninterrupted system operation.

Architecture

Topology: Active ideal diode OR-ing

Redundancy: N+1 source redundancy

Switching: Seamless, zero-interruption transfer

Performance

Efficiency: >99% power path efficiency

Voltage Drop: <20 mV @ 20 A

Thermal: Low-loss, passive operation

Intelligent Control

Features: Automatic source prioritization, Reverse current blocking, and Health-aware power arbitration.

Why Active OR-ing?

Traditional passive diodes waste power as heat due to forward voltage drop. Eonix Flow replaces these with active MOSFET-based ideal diode controllers, reducing losses to near zero and enabling high-current redundancy without thermal penalties.

• Reverse Current Blocking: Prevents battery backfeeding into weak sources.

• Source Prioritization: Intelligently drain auxiliary batteries before main packs.

• Stable Switching: Built-in hysteresis prevents oscillation between similar sources.

• Smart Protection: Integrated Reverse Polarity and Load Dump protection for rugged environments.

Eonix Cell

Eonix Cell is the intelligent battery management module of the Eonix ecosystem. It monitors, protects, and controls connected battery packs, enabling safe charging, controlled discharging, and real-time visibility into state-of-charge, health, and system status.

Battery Management

Features: Multi-cell battery monitoring, Controlled charge/discharge paths, and Active Cell Balancing for extended pack life.

Real-Time Insights

Telemetry: State-of-charge (SoC) estimation, State-of-health (SoH) tracking, plus Voltage, Current, and Temperature monitoring.

Smart Power Control

Control: On-demand charge or discharge control, Automatic source/load coordination, and Fault detection with isolation.

Intelligent Battery Integration

Eonix Cell transforms battery packs into active, intelligent power nodes. Instead of acting as passive energy storage, the module continuously monitors cell conditions, calculates state-of-charge, and coordinates power flow based on system demands.

• Active Protection: Hardware-enforced over-current, over-voltage, and under-voltage safeguards.

• Accurate SoC Tracking: Real-time charge estimation based on current integration and voltage models.

• Health Monitoring: Tracks temperature, cycle behavior, and battery condition over time.

• Smart Safety: Integrated Thermal Management and Deep Discharge Prevention.
